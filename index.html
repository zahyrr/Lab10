From TV to Web Videos

Remember when the whole family sat together in a room focusing on a bulky boxed machine with a huge dial that allowed us to watch only what was on, families shared this experience by getting together in a room, all looking towards this box and listening to the sounds coming out of its speakers. You had to turn it on and the device brought in information and entertainment into our lives. This device is called a television and the moving pictures with sounds is called a video.

The television over the years has been replaced by mobile phones or other smart devices and the videos are delivered through internet, making the viewing experience of videos more personal. With the amount of videos out there, everyone has their own preferences when it comes to web videos.

Video Comsumption Today

In 2020, we control "what?", "how?" and "when?" we watch web videos through devices such as smart phones, tablets or other mobile devices and now we even have features where we can control these devices just through our voice or hand gestures, making videos more and more accesible and convinient.Video content and its uses today have taken a huge leap from where they once were, now the viewers can find and watch the type of content they truly enjoy, there are several options out there both paid and free.The experience today has changed and brought in an element of freedom to choose what we want to watch. We can watch things that we actually care about and not just that, we can even watch it "When we want?" and "Where we want?".

Trends in web videos seemed like an interesting topic to explore, I did this by going through several articles that were available on the Internet.

Video Trends You Need to Know About In 2020:

  • Live Videos:Live streaming videos on social media can be used by brands and companies to interact and engage their consumers through Q&As, Ask me anything or live demos.
  • 360 Degree Videos:360 degree videos make the viewing more immersive, engaging and interactive.
  • Training/Informative/Educational Videos:Videos today can be used for training and education purpose and can reach consumer across the world without them having to leave the comfort of their homes.
  • VR Videos:Virtual reality takes the viewing experience to another level by engaging the consumer completely with little to no distractions.
  • Mobile Video Advertising:Due to the number of users that consume content using a mobile device the amount of videos produced for mobile devices will increase as these are smaller in size, which can allow a one on one interaction
